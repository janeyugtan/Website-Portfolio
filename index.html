My Story

Working remotely since 2021

I started working remotely in 2021, and in 2022 I landed my first major project supporting a U.S.-based marketing agency. That experience shaped the direction of my career.

Since then, I’ve supported founders and agencies across operations, systems, project delivery, and client management — working with around 10 clients and indirectly supporting 100+ of their clients.

I currently work as an Account Manager for an e-learning provider, supporting client continuity, renewal coordination, and operational improvements.

I do my best work in roles that create clarity, save time, and help teams move faster without sacrificing quality.
