About

How I got started and what I am focused on now.

My Story

Working remotely since 2021

I started working remotely in 2021. In 2022 I got my first real project with a marketing agency in the US and everything changed from there.

Since then I have worked directly with around 10 clients, mostly founders and agency owners. Through their businesses, I have touched 100+ client accounts across delivery, systems, and ops work.

This includes enterprise account management at a US-based company serving Fortune 500 clients — work that sharpened how I handle high-volume, high-stakes relationships at scale.

Now I focus on working with founders who want things running properly without micromanaging every moving part.

Current Focus

What I am working on now

My day-to-day work keeps me sharp across project delivery, operational support, and systems work. That is exactly what I bring to the projects I take on here.

I take on retainer-based and project work when the scope makes sense, whether that is running ongoing operations, fixing broken workflows, or building systems people actually use.

If the scope is right and the timing works, I would love to connect.

Life Outside the Laptop

Cats, travels, and sunsets

I have two British Shorthairs. Pochi is my resident cat, a blue blotched smoke who is genuinely emotionally intelligent. He comes when called and always knows when I need a break. Asher is my pure cinnamon newcomer and has fully taken over the energy in the house.
